# Enterprise Inventory Management System


## Project Overview

A production-style SQL Server based Inventory Management System.

### Master Data

- Product
- Category
- Brand
- Supplier


### Purchase

- Supplier Management
- Purchase Orders
- Goods Receiving


### Inventory

- Warehouse Management
- Stock Tracking
- Stock Adjustment
- Stock Transfer


### Sales

- Customer Management
- Sales Orders
- Shipment Processing


### Reporting

- Inventory Reports
- Sales Reports
- Purchase Reports


### Audit

- Product Change Tracking
- Inventory Change Tracking
